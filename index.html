Pretzel and Hotdog have lived their whole life in a cart. They decided to escape when their friend got sold
On their adventure they came across a log with an ant. They became friends.
they continued down the park road and came across a rock. Flying around the rock was a bee. The bee then joined them on their journey.
The four new friends all continued down the path. After a few minutes they came across a leaf with a small snail. They started talking and the snail joined their group.
After a little bit of time Pretzel noticed a figure that looked like him. They looked a little closer and realized infront of them was a group of hotdogs and pretzels just like them.
They all became friends and decided to start a town so they could all live happily, freely, and safely. And that's what they did.
The new family is still living in their town peacefully and they will continue to live there forever. THE END!
